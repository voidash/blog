  • Veritasium video “You’ve been playing the game of life wrong” goes through “Power curve Vs Normal curve”. Events that have additive effects follow the gaussian distribution like height, exam scores etc. Events that are multiplicative where event build on top of each where events compound, where extremes dominate totals follow the power distribution like Earthquakes, Forest fires, Wealth distribution to views on youtube videos. he main highlight point of the video is"persistence(tenacity) vs consistence(uniformity)". . Keep on showing up and trying bunch of things until you are lucky enough to basket one rare extreme event. One such example is VCs persistently putting money on various domains knowing all the investment is going to be absorbed by one lucky company that succeeds. This is why one paper snowballs a researcher, one video defines a Youtuber career.

    I always used to ponder why people used to over attribute success over skill, but these were the smart people who knew Talent != outcome, effort != reward, fairness !=equality. They knew it’s persistently looking for luck by showing up.

    My tail event will come because i vow that 2026 is going to be year of me being in places i am not yet qualified for and i plan to do it all the way down.
